## 💰 Payment Methods That Actually Work in Nepal

> ⚠️ **PayPal does NOT work for receiving money in Nepal.** Don't waste time on it.

| Method | Status | Best For | How to Setup |
|--------|--------|----------|--------------|
| **Payoneer** | ✅ Best Option | Upwork, Fiverr, Direct Clients | Link with Nabil/Global IME bank |
| **Skrill + eSewa** | ✅ Works | Freelancer.com users | Skrill → eSewa → Bank (1.45% fee) |
| **Wise (client sends)** | ✅ Works | Direct international clients | Client sends to your Nepali bank via SWIFT |
| **SWIFT Bank Transfer** | ✅ Works | Large payments ($2000+) | 3-7 days, higher fees |
| **eSewa / Khalti** | ✅ Local | Local clients, domestic work | Instant local transfers |
| **Dollar Card** | ✅ Works | Paying for online tools | Nabil, Global IME, NMB bank ($500/year limit) |

### 🏦 Recommended Bank Setup
1. **Primary:** Nabil Bank or Global IME Bank account
2. **Get a Payoneer account** linked to your bank
3. **Get eSewa/Khalti** for local transactions
4. **Optional:** Prepaid Dollar Card for tool subscriptions

## 🛡️ Safety Rules — Avoid Scams

| NEVER Do This | ALWAYS Do This |
|----------------|-------------------|
| Pay money to get a job | Legitimate platforms are FREE to join |
| Share bank PIN/password | Use escrow payment systems |
| Trust "Earn $1000/day" ads | Start with small, realistic goals |
| Use VPN for fake country | Build a real profile in Nepal |
| Accept unknown payment links | Use Payoneer, Skrill, or bank transfer |
| Work without a contract | Always have written agreements |
